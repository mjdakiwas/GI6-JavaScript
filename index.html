Ready to test your luck?

You and the computer will each randomly receive 10-250 dollars, however you do not know what amount your opponent will receive and vice versa.

Each round of Rock, Paper, Scissors, you and the computer secretly decide to continue or stop.

If you win a round of RPS, you will gain 10 dollars.

Each round cost 10 dollars to play.

You and your opponent can keep playing or either player can "put down" their hand to stop the game, or one runs out of money.

Your goal is to have more money than your opponent once either player have "put down" their hand or the game is over.
